Easy & Healthy Moringa Soup Recipe

Enjoy a delicious and nutritious Moringa Soup! This simple recipe uses fresh moringa leaves and common ingredients, making it a perfect, budget-friendly meal for the whole family. Learn about the amazing health benefits of moringa.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup fresh moringa leaves
  • 4 cups water or vegetable broth (for added flavor)
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 1 small tomato, chopped
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil or coconut oil (for added health benefits)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: A pinch of turmeric for added color and anti-inflammatory benefits

Instructions

  1. Heat the oil in a pot over medium heat.
  2. Sauté the onion and garlic until fragrant.
  3. Add the chopped tomato and cook until softened.
  4. Pour in the water or broth and bring to a boil.
  5. Reduce the heat, add the moringa leaves, and simmer for 5-7 minutes, or until the leaves are tender.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Add turmeric if using.
  7. Serve hot and enjoy the goodness!

Nutrition Information (Approximate)

Per Serving: Calories 60, Protein 2g, Fat 2g, Carbohydrates 8g (Note: These values are estimates and may vary depending on ingredient amounts.)
