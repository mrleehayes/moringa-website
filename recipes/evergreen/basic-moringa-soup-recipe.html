Nutritious Basic Moringa Soup Recipe

This easy and budget-friendly moringa soup recipe is a great way to enjoy the health benefits of moringa leaves. Perfect for low-income families looking for simple yet nutritious meals.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup fresh moringa leaves
  • 4 cups water
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 1 small tomato, chopped
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat the vegetable oil in a pot over medium heat.
  2. Saut√© the onion and garlic until fragrant.
  3. Add the chopped tomato and cook until softened.
  4. Pour in the water and bring to a boil.
  5. Reduce the heat, add the moringa leaves, and simmer for 5-7 minutes.
  6. Season with salt to taste and serve hot.

Nutrition Information

Per Serving: Calories 60, Protein 2g, Fat 2g, Carbohydrates 8g.
