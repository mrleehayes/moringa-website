Moringa Smoothie for Energy (No Banana)

Moringa Smoothie for Energy (No Banana)

If you’re looking for a banana-free energy smoothie, this moringa powder recipe is refreshing, nutritious, and easy to digest. Packed with plant-based protein, iron, and antioxidants, it’s the perfect morning drink for focus and stamina.

Watch the Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 tsp moringa powder
  • 1 cup unsweetened almond milk (or oat milk)
  • ½ cup frozen mango or pineapple
  • 1 tbsp chia seeds
  • 1 tbsp almond or peanut butter
  • 1–2 tsp honey or maple syrup (optional)
  • Ice cubes (optional)

Instructions

  1. Add all ingredients to a blender.
  2. Blend until smooth and creamy (about 30–60 seconds).
  3. Taste and adjust sweetness or thickness as desired.
  4. Pour into a glass and enjoy immediately.

Tips & Variations

  • Add a scoop of plant-based protein powder for a protein boost.
  • Use coconut water instead of almond milk for extra hydration.
  • Swap mango for berries if you prefer a tangier flavor.
  • Find more ideas on easy ways to add moringa to your diet.

Nutrition Information (Approximate)

  • Calories: 220
  • Protein: 6g
  • Fiber: 6g
  • Iron: 15% RDA (Learn more about moringa benefits!)

FAQs

Can I make this ahead of time?
Yes! Store in a sealed jar for up to 24 hours. Shake before drinking.

What does moringa powder taste like?
It has an earthy, slightly grassy flavor. Tropical fruit helps mask the bitterness. Read more about what moringa is to understand its profile.
