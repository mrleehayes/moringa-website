A simple and nutritious way to enjoy the health benefits of moringa every day.

Make Moringa Tea at Home

This simple and nutritious herbal tea is rich in antioxidants and essential nutrients. A perfect drink for boosting your health naturally!

Watch the Recipe

Ingredients

  • Fresh moringa leaves (as needed)
  • Hot water (for brewing)
  • Optional: Honey, lemon, or ginger for extra flavor

Instructions

  1. Harvest and Clean: Pick fresh moringa leaves and rinse thoroughly.
  2. Air Dry: Hang the leaves in a shaded, well-ventilated area for 3-5 days until completely dry.
  3. Pan-Dry (Optional): Heat a dry pan over low heat and stir the leaves for 5-10 minutes to remove moisture.
  4. Crush and Store: Once dried, crush the leaves and store them in an airtight container or tea bags.
  5. Brew Your Tea: Add 1-2 teaspoons of dried moringa leaves (or a tea bag) to a cup. Pour hot (not boiling) water and steep for 5-10 minutes before straining.
  6. Enjoy! Serve as is, or add honey, lemon, or ginger for extra flavor.
