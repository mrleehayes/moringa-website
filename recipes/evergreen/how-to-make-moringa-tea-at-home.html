How to Make Moringa Tea

This simple and nutritious herbal tea is packed with antioxidants and essential nutrients. Learn how to make moringa tea at home and enjoy its numerous health benefits!

Watch the Moringa Tea Recipe Video

Moringa Tea Ingredients

  • Fresh moringa leaves (or dried moringa leaves)
  • Hot water
  • Optional: Honey, lemon, ginger, or other spices

How to Make Moringa Tea

  1. Prepare the Moringa Leaves: If using fresh leaves, rinse them thoroughly. If using dried, you can skip this step.
  2. Brew the Tea: Add 1-2 teaspoons of fresh or dried moringa leaves to a cup. Pour hot (not boiling) water over the leaves.
  3. Steep: Let the tea steep for 5-10 minutes, depending on your desired strength.
  4. Strain (Optional): Strain the tea to remove the leaves, if desired.
  5. Serve & Enjoy: Your moringa tea is ready! Add honey, lemon, ginger, or other spices to taste.

Health Benefits of Moringa Tea

Moringa tea is a powerhouse of nutrients and antioxidants. Some potential benefits include:

  • Rich in vitamins and minerals
  • High in antioxidants
  • May help boost immunity
  • Could have anti-inflammatory properties
