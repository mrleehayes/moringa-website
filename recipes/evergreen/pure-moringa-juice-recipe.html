Pure Moringa Juice

A simple, nutrient-packed moringa juice recipe to enjoy as a refreshing drink or use in smoothies, soups, and stews.

Pure Moringa Juice

This simple and nutritious moringa recipe is ideal for refreshing drinks, smoothies, and stews.

Watch the Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup fresh moringa leaves (stripped from stems)
  • 1 cup water (adjust as needed)

Instructions

  1. Strip fresh moringa leaves from the stems.
  2. Use a pestle and mortar to crush the leaves into a paste.
  3. Gradually mix in the water.
  4. Transfer to a blender and blend until smooth.
  5. Use a fine mesh strainer or cheesecloth to separate the juice from the pulp.
  6. Drink immediately, freeze into ice cubes for smoothies, or store as concentrated cubes for stews and soups.

Nutrition Information

Per Serving: Calories 10, Protein 1g, Fat 0g, Carbohydrates 2g.
