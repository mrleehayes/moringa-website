Immunity Boosting Moringa, Orange, Carrot & Garlic Juice

This vibrant and refreshing juice is packed with vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants to help strengthen your immune system and fight off colds and flu. Learn more about moringa's amazing benefits.

Watch the Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup fresh Moringa leaves (packed)
  • 2 large Oranges, peeled
  • 2 medium Carrots, peeled and chopped
  • 2 cloves Garlic, peeled
  • 1 cup Water (filtered is best)
  • Optional: Honey or ginger to taste (adjust for sweetness/spice)

Ingredient Notes & Substitutions

  • No Fresh Moringa? No problem! Substitute 1 tablespoon of high-quality moringa powder for the fresh leaves. Add it directly to the blender with the other ingredients.
  • Blender vs. Juicer: This recipe is designed for a blender to retain all the healthy fiber. If using a juicer, you'll get a thinner juice. Simply juice the oranges, carrots, and garlic, then stir in the moringa powder (or blend the juiced liquid with fresh moringa leaves).
  • Taste: The garlic gives this juice a savory kick. If you're new to it, start with just one small clove.

Instructions

  1. Thoroughly wash the Moringa leaves, oranges, carrots, and garlic. Chop the carrots into smaller pieces for easier blending.
  2. Add all ingredients (Moringa, oranges, carrots, garlic, and water) to a high-speed blender. Blend until smooth.
  3. If you prefer a smoother juice without pulp, strain the mixture through a fine-mesh sieve or cheesecloth. This step removes the fibrous parts of the moringa and any remaining orange pulp.
  4. Pour the juice into glasses and enjoy immediately for maximum nutrient absorption. You can add ice if desired.

Frequently Asked Questions

What does this moringa juice taste like?

It has a refreshing, earthy base from the moringa and carrot, a bright citrus sweetness from the orange, and a pungent, savory kick from the garlic. It's a complex and surprisingly delicious health shot!

Can I make this juice ahead of time?

For maximum nutritional value, it's best consumed immediately. However, you can store it in an airtight container (like a mason jar) in the refrigerator for up to 24 hours. The ingredients may separate, so give it a good shake before drinking.

How often should I drink this?

A small glass daily or every other day is a great way to support your immune system, especially during cold and flu season. Listen to your body and consult a healthcare professional if you have underlying health conditions.
